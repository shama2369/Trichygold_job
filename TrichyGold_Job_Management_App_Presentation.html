üìù How to Create a New Job

  1. Click "Add New" from the main menu
  2. Fill in Job Details:
    • Job ID (auto-generated)
    • Job Name
    • Description
    • Entry Date (auto-filled with today's date)
    • Planned Start Date
    • Status (Active, Planned, Suggested, Completed, Cancelled)
  3. Assign Employees:
    • Click "Add Employee" to assign team members
    • Select from the dropdown list
    • You can assign multiple employees to one job
    • Each employee can only be assigned once per job
  4. Click "Add New" to save the job
‚úÖ Success: Once created, all assigned employees will receive a WhatsApp notification about the new job assignment!
